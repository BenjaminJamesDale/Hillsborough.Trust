A Bit About Us

Hillsborough Trust Museum is a museum located in Sheffield, England that has exhibits on prehistoric times, Hillsborough history, industrial revolution and steel city, and a dynamic exhibit that changes on a monthly basis1. The museum is operated by Sheffield Museums Trust.

The prehistoric exhibit showcases Sheffield’s history from the Stone Age to the Iron Age. The Hillsborough history exhibit showcases the history of Hillsborough from its early days to present times. The industrial revolution and steel city exhibit showcases Sheffield’s industrial heritage and how it became known as the Steel City3. The dynamic exhibit changes on a monthly basis and covers various topics.
